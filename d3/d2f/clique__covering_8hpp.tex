\subsection{Detailed Description}
This header file includes four different algorithms heuristically solving the clique covering problem. 

This header has 4 classes solving clique covering problem. Three of them consider the weighted of the edges during the clique selection.

The first two heuristics are based on these two papers\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item E. Tomita, A. Tanaka, H. Takahashi \char`\"{}\+The worst-\/case time complexity for generating all maximal cliques and computational experiments\char`\"{}, Theoretical Computer Science, Volume 363, Issue 1, 2006.
\item K. C. Dukka Bahadur, Tatsuya Akutsu, Etsuji Tomita, and Tomokazu Seki. \char`\"{}\+Protein side-\/chain packing problem\+: a maximum edge-\/weight clique algorithmic approach\char`\"{}, In Proceedings of the second conference on Asia-\/\+Pacific bioinformatics -\/ Volume 29 (A\+P\+BC \textquotesingle{}04), Yi-\/\+Ping Phoebe Chen (Ed.), Vol. 29. Australian Computer Society, Inc., Darlinghurst, Australia, Australia, 191-\/200. The third one is a simple clique covering obtained by coloring the complement graph. The last one performs iteratively the clique covering on a filtered graph induced by different edge weights.
\end{DoxyItemize}
